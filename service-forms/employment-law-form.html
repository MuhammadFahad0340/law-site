Employment Law Services

Ensuring fairness and justice in the workplace for employees and employers

Our Key Responsibilities in Employment Law

Wrongful Termination

Protect employees from unlawful dismissal and ensure proper procedures are followed in termination cases.

Discrimination & Harassment

Combat workplace discrimination and harassment based on protected characteristics and create safe work environments.

Wage & Hour Disputes

Ensure fair compensation, overtime pay, and compliance with wage and hour laws for all workers.

Employment Contracts

Draft, review, and negotiate employment agreements, non-compete clauses, and severance packages.

Workplace Safety

Ensure compliance with OSHA regulations and protect workers' rights to a safe working environment.

Labor Relations

Handle union negotiations, collective bargaining agreements, and labor dispute resolution.
